# Privacy Policy — UTC/Unix Clock

**Last updated:** 2026-03-01

## Data Collection

UTC/Unix Clock does **not** collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

## Local Storage

The extension stores a single preference (your selected timezone) in your browser's local storage. This data:

- Never leaves your device
- Is not sent to any server
- Is not shared with any third party
- Can be cleared by removing the extension

## Permissions

The extension requests **no browser permissions**.

## Contact

For questions about this policy, open an issue at https://github.com/zhongdai/chrome-utc-now/issues
